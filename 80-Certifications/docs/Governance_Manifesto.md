# Certification & Governance Manifesto: Framework Standardization (ADD-80)

## 1. Executive Summary
Certifications are more than badges; they represent standardized languages for governance, risk management, and service delivery. We utilize these frameworks to align our engineering processes with global enterprise standards, ensuring predictability and accountability across the project lifecycle.

## 2. Core Framework Pillars
- **PMI (Project Management Institute):** Standards for project delivery, scope management, and stakeholder engagement.
- **ITIL (IT Infrastructure Library):** Framework for IT Service Management (ITSM), focusing on service delivery and operational efficiency.
- **Prince2 (Projects IN Controlled Environments):** Process-based approach for effective project management and control.
- **RUP (Rational Unified Process):** A disciplined iterative software development process framework.

## 3. Governance Strategy
- **Framework Tailoring:** We do not apply frameworks strictly (out-of-the-box); we tailor them to the specific organizational context to avoid bureaucratic overhead.
- **Continuous Compliance:** Integration of governance checks into the CI/CD pipeline, ensuring every release meets the established standards (e.g., Change Management requirements).
