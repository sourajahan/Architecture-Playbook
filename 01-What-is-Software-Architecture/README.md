 # 01: The Architect's Manifesto

> *"Code is cheap. Architecture is expensive. If you get it wrong, you don't just fix a bug—you rebuild the empire."*

---

## 🏛️ IDENTITY: Who is the Architect?
> **The Architect is the Guardian of Vision.**
> You are not a glorified coder. You are the intersection between **Business Ambition** and **Technical Reality**.
> 
> *   **Strategic Thinker:** You look at the system as a living organism, not just a set of features.
> *   **Risk Manager:** You identify the structural flaws before they crash the product.
> *   **Decision Maker:** You own the choices that are too expensive to revert.
