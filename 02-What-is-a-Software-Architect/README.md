# 02: The Anatomy of a Software Architect

> "A Software Architect is the force that prevents a project from becoming a 'Big Ball of Mud.' You don't just build systems; you own their future."

---

## 🧐 The Identity: Who are you?
The industry confuses a **Senior Developer** with an **Architect**. Let’s clear the air:
*   **The Developer** solves the *local* problem.
*   **The Architect** solves the *systemic* problem.

You are the one who ensures the code doesn't just work today, but remains maintainable, scalable, and secure in three years.
